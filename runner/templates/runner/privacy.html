Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Data We Collect

CLUTCH RUN collects only the information you voluntarily provide: your chosen callsign (username) and your game scores. We do not collect email addresses, passwords, or any personally-identifiable information.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your callsign and scores are stored solely for the purpose of maintaining the in-game global leaderboard and displaying your personal statistics. We do not sell, share, or distribute your data to any third party.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored in a server-side SQLite database. User preferences (e.g. sound settings, bloom toggle) are stored locally in your browser via localStorage and never leave your device.

4. Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your score history by using the DELETE /api/player/<username>/reset/ endpoint. This permanently removes all your score records from the database.

5. Cookies

CLUTCH RUN does not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics services. The only cookies used are standard Django CSRF tokens required for secure form submissions.

6. Contact

For questions about this policy, open an issue on our GitHub repository.
